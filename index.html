WELCOME TO MY SITE

Food technology is the application of food science to the selection, preservation, processing, packaging, distribution, and use of safe, nutritious, and wholesome food. It encompasses a wide range of activities, including research and development, quality control, and production management. Food technologists use their knowledge of the chemical, physical, and biological properties of food to develop new products, improve existing ones, and ensure that they are safe and of high quality. They may work in a variety of settings, including research laboratories, food processing plants, and government agencies. Some of the key areas of focus in food technology include food safety, food processing and preservation, food packaging, and the development of new food products.

HACCP LEVEL 3

The HACCP Level 3 Certificate is a professional qualification that demonstrates a person's knowledge and understanding of Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point (HACCP) principles and practices. This certificate is designed for individuals working in food safety and quality control positions, as well as those responsible for implementing and maintaining HACCP systems in their organization..

FOSTAG LEVEL 3

The FOSTAG Level 3 Certificate is a qualification that demonstrates a person's knowledge and skills in the field of food safety. It is typically earned through completing a training program or course that covers topics such as food safety regulations, hazard identification and control, and food handling practices.
